About Dadigamuwa

Nestled in the Colombo District, Dadigamuwa is a vibrant village that embodies the rural essence of Sri Lanka. Surrounded by lush rice paddies, coconut plantations, and serene waterways, it offers a peaceful contrast to urban life.

Dadigamuwa Temple

The heart of the village's spiritual life, the Dadigamuwa Temple stands as a symbol of peace and cultural preservation. Surrounded by lush vegetation, it serves as a central gathering point for community celebrations and religious practices.
